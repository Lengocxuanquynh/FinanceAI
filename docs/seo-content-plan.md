# SEO Content Plan (Post-Setup)

## 1) Muc tieu SEO theo giai doan

- Week 1: Index nhanh 100% URL quan trong, co click dau tien, 5-10 keyword vao top 30.
- Week 2-4: Day cum long-tail vao top 10, tang CTR qua title/meta A/B.
- Month 2+: Chot cum money keyword, xay authority qua digital PR va backlink chat luong.

## 2) Kien truc noi dung theo Topic Cluster

- Pillar 1: AI Personal Finance Dashboard
  - Cluster: AI budgeting, spending analytics, monthly savings plan
- Pillar 2: Crypto + Stock Portfolio Management
  - Cluster: risk allocation, rebalance strategy, volatility tracking
- Pillar 3: Expense Optimization
  - Cluster: recurring bill audit, category optimization, expense anomaly detection

## 3) Lich dang bai 4 tuan

- Tuan 1:
  - 1 bai pillar + 4 bai cluster
  - 2 bai blog comparison intent (vs alternatives)
- Tuan 2:
  - 1 bai pillar + 5 bai cluster
  - 1 landing page transactional intent
- Tuan 3:
  - 4 bai cluster + 2 case-study
- Tuan 4:
  - Update 6 bai co impression cao, them FAQ schema, toi uu internal links

## 4) Mau workflow bai post

1. Keyword mapping (primary + 3-5 semantic keywords).
2. Viet outline theo search intent.
3. Publish voi schema Article + FAQ.
4. Link ve Pillar va 2 bai cluster lien quan.
5. Submit URL tren Google Search Console.
6. Review sau 72h: impression, CTR, vi tri.

## 5) Quy tac technical SEO bat buoc

- URL ngan, khong dau, on dinh.
- Moi bai co title unique, meta description unique.
- Canonical dung tren tat ca trang.
- Internal links toi thieu 3 link/bai.
- Anh upload Cloudinary co alt text theo keyword.

## 6) KPI dashboard can theo doi hang ngay

- So URL duoc index
- Impression theo cum keyword
- Top 3 / Top 10 keyword count
- CTR theo query
- Conversion tu blog sang sign-up
